Coming to the conference

Polytech is ~20min from the center of Lille using the metro line 1 ("4 Cantons Stade Pierre Mauroy" stop). The center of Lille or the "Vieux Lille" district in the north are quiet good areas for your hotel due to the beauty of these districts and the quantity/quality of restaurants and bars available there ;-)

Coming to Lille from Paris or Brussels

Important: note that these trains require reservation, book your ticket ahead of time!
To book trains in Belgium : https://www.b-europe.com/EN
To book trains in France : https://en.oui.sncf/en/

from Paris, Gare du Nord train Station:

take TGV train to Lille Europe or Lille Flandres stations : 1 hour

from Paris airport (Roissy Charles de Gaulle airport (CDG))

take TGV train at Charles de Gaulle 2 train station inside the airport
to Lille Europe station : 50min.

from Brussels airport

use a national train to reach Brussels Midi station (30-40min) and then ...

from Brussels Midi train station

take TGV or Thalys or Eurostar train to Lille Europe station : 35min
